App title
SafeNight

Short description
Walking navigation with safer route insights, live location sharing, and safety features.

Full description
SafeNight is a walking navigation app designed to help people make safer journey choices at night.

Key features:
- Route safety scoring to compare options before you start walking.
- Street-light and crime-context overlays to support route decisions.
- Turn-by-turn walking navigation.
- Live location sharing with trusted contacts.
- Safety Circle tools to help contacts follow your journey.

SafeNight is intended to support personal safety decisions. It does not guarantee safety in any location.
